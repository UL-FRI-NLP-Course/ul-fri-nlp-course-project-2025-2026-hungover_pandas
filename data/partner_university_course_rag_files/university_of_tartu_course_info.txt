EXCHANGE STUDENT COURSE SELECTION

The University of Tartu offers international students English-taught courses in different disciplines. International visiting students register for courses through the Study Information System (SIS) within the first two weeks of the semester.

Course information is available through the University of Tartu course systems and Study Information System.

COMPUTER SCIENCE RELEVANCE

The University of Tartu has a strong Computer Science profile. Its Computer Science master's programme includes specializations such as:
- cryptography and verification,
- distributed systems,
- artificial intelligence.

Other relevant topics may include:
- data mining,
- data science,
- parallel computing,
- cloud computing,
- cyber security,
- secure programming,
- software engineering,
- and theoretical computer science.

Exchange students should use the official course information systems to check the current English-taught courses and whether they are available to international visiting students.

IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS

Course availability depends on:
- semester,
- language of instruction,
- prerequisites,
- whether the course is open to international visiting students,
- and the Study Information System registration process.

Students must register for courses in the SIS after receiving their University of Tartu credentials.

SAFE ANSWER BEHAVIOR

If a student asks about Tartu, the assistant should recommend checking the official University of Tartu English-taught course list, the Study Information System, and the Institute of Computer Science course pages.

Example answer:
“University of Tartu is a strong option for Computer Science topics such as AI, distributed systems, cryptography, cyber security, and data science. You should verify current English-taught courses in the University of Tartu SIS and include approved choices in your Learning Agreement.”
