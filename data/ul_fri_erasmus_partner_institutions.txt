UL FRI ERASMUS+ PARTNER INSTITUTIONS
Academic year: 2026/27
Source: UL FRI Erasmus Office
Last updated: May 2026

This document contains Erasmus+ partner institutions available to students of the Faculty of Computer and Information Science (UL FRI).

For each institution, the following information is provided:
- institution name
- Erasmus code
- country
- number of students accepted per academic year
- mobility duration in months per student
- study levels:
  1 = Bachelor
  2 = Master
  3 = PhD

GENERAL NOTES

Study is generally conducted in the language of the host country, especially at Bachelor level. Some institutions also offer courses and exams in English.

Students should carefully check:
- the institution’s website,
- course catalogs,
- language requirements,
- and whether the institution offers courses relevant to their study programme before applying.

Some institutions may require:
- a signed bilateral agreement,
- or available free places for mobility.
