ERASMUS+ SHORT-TERM MOBILITY FOR DOCTORAL STUDENTS AT UL FRI

Published: May 2026

Within the Erasmus+ programme, doctoral students at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science (UL FRI) may complete part of their regular study obligations, practical training, research work, or doctoral thesis preparation at one of UL FRI’s Erasmus+ partner institutions.

Short-term doctoral mobility is possible:
- at Erasmus+ partner institutions,
- in partner countries from regions 13 and 14,
- and in third countries not associated with the Erasmus+ programme.

The eligible mobility period for this call is:
1 June 2026 – 30 June 2027,
or until available funding is exhausted.

APPLICATION DEADLINE

Students must submit the application form and all mandatory attachments at least 1.5 months before the start of mobility.

APPLICATION PROCEDURE

Students apply by submitting:
- the application form,
- and all required attachments

via email to:
erasmus@fri.uni-lj.si

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Questions and answers regarding doctoral Erasmus+ mobility are available on the University of Ljubljana website:
https://www.uni-lj.si/studij/izmenjave/izmenjave-erasmus/izmenjave-za-doktorske-studente/pogosta-vprasanja


ERASMUS+ TRAINEESHIP MOBILITY FOR STUDENTS

Published: August 2025

UL FRI students may complete practical traineeships within the Erasmus+ programme.

The eligible period for this call is:
1 October 2025 – 30 September 2026.

Applications are accepted until:
- available funds are exhausted,
or
- no later than 1 July 2026.

MOBILITY DURATION

The total funding limit is up to 12 months for study and/or traineeship mobility combined.

Students may complete multiple traineeship mobilities if:
- they are not with the same employer or organization,
- each traineeship lasts at least 2 months,
- and no traineeship exceeds 6 months.

APPLICATION DEADLINE

Students must submit the complete application with all mandatory attachments at least 2 months before the start of mobility.

Applications are submitted via email to:
erasmus@fri.uni-lj.si

Students applying for mobility after graduation or after completing a master’s degree must submit the application at least 2 months before the expiration of their student status and thesis defense.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

Students must submit:
- completed application form,
- signed and stamped Erasmus+ Learning Agreement for Traineeship,
- motivation letter (minimum 1 page, maximum 2 pages, Slovenian language allowed).

The application and all required documents must be submitted electronically to:
erasmus@fri.uni-lj.si
