Q1: What happens after I am accepted for an Erasmus exchange?
A: FRI nominates you to the partner university, and you must apply to the host institution and for Erasmus+ funding.

Q2: What is a Learning Agreement?
A: It is a document that defines which courses you will take abroad and how they will be recognized at FRI.

Q3: Who must approve the Learning Agreement?
A: The student, the FRI coordinator, and the host institution coordinator.

Q4: When must I submit my Erasmus+ funding application?
A: At least 10 days before the start of your mobility.

Q5: How many ECTS do I need to complete during Erasmus?
A: At least 20 ECTS per semester (or 40 for a full year).

Q6: Can I take language courses during Erasmus?
A: Yes, as part of general elective courses.

Q7: Can I complete mandatory FRI courses remotely while on Erasmus?
A: No, mandatory courses cannot be completed remotely during the exchange.

Q8: How is the Erasmus scholarship paid?
A: 80% is paid before the exchange and the remaining 20% after completion.

Q9: What is the minimum and maximum duration of an Erasmus exchange?
A: Minimum 2 months, maximum 12 months.

Q10: Do I pay tuition fees at the host university?
A: No, Erasmus students do not pay tuition, exams, or lab usage fees.

Q11: Are there any additional costs at the host university?
A: Yes, small fees for insurance, student organizations, or materials may apply.

Q12: Is language testing mandatory before Erasmus?
A: No, OLS testing is optional, but some universities may require certificates.

Q13: Can I do my thesis during Erasmus?
A: Yes, but only at some institutions and with both FRI and host mentors.

Q14: What happens if I fail some courses abroad?
A: Recognition depends on how similar the completed courses are to FRI requirements.

Q15: What documents do I need after returning from Erasmus?
A: You must submit documents via the UL portal and the Studis system.

Q16: Who can help me if I have problems during Erasmus?
A: The host coordinator, FRI coordinator, Erasmus mentor, UL office, or student tutors.

Q17: When do I need to submit documents after returning?
A: Within 3 months after the exchange or before the end of the academic year.

Q18: Are there extra financial supports available?
A: Yes, students with fewer opportunities can receive additional funding.
