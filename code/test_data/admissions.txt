UL FRI Admission Requirements for International Students

International students who wish to study at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science (FRI) at the University of Ljubljana must meet the following requirements.

For the Master's programme in Computer Science, applicants must hold a Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field. The programme is taught in English.

Non-EU students must apply through the University of Ljubljana international office. The application deadline for non-EU students is March 1st each year. EU students may apply until May 15th.

Required documents include: certified copy of your Bachelor's diploma, transcript of records, proof of English language proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90), a motivation letter, and a copy of your passport.

Tuition fees for non-EU students are approximately 2500 EUR per year for the Master's programme. EU students do not pay tuition fees.
