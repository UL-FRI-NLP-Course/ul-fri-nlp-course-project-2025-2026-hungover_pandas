UL FRI ERASMUS EXCHANGE PROCESS AND REQUIREMENTS

After being selected for an Erasmus exchange, the following steps occur:

1. Nomination:
FRI nominates the student to the selected partner institution.
The University of Ljubljana prepares Erasmus+ financial support.

2. Application to host institution:
The host university sends instructions on how to apply, including:
- required documents
- deadlines
- accommodation information

3. Erasmus+ grant application:
Students must apply through the UL online system.
Deadline: at least 10 days before mobility starts.
Required documents:
- Letter of Acceptance
- Online Learning Agreement (OLA)
- Proof of enrollment
- Bank account details

LEARNING AGREEMENT (LA / OLA)

The Learning Agreement defines which courses the student will take abroad and how they will be recognized at FRI.

Requirements:
- Must be completed BEFORE departure
- Must include course descriptions (links)
- Must be approved by:
  - student
  - FRI coordinator
  - host institution coordinator

Two systems:
- OLA (Online Learning Agreement – standard system)
- FRI platform (if host is not in OLA system)

COURSE SELECTION RULES

- Elective courses: any CS-related subject abroad
- General electives: can include language courses
- Mandatory courses: must match content
- Cannot complete mandatory FRI courses remotely during exchange

ECTS REQUIREMENTS

- Minimum: 20 ECTS per semester (required for scholarship)
- 40 ECTS for full year
- If courses are not fully completed, recognition depends on content similarity

FINAL THESIS ABROAD

- Possible but not at all institutions
- Requires:
  - mentor at FRI
  - mentor at host institution
- Must be included in Learning Agreement
- ECTS:
  - Bachelor thesis: 6 ECTS
  - Master thesis: 24 ECTS

ERASMUS SCHOLARSHIP

Application:
- via UL portal
- opens by June 1
- must be completed at least 10 days before departure

Payment:
- 80% before mobility
- 20% after completion

Conditions:
- active student status
- no outstanding obligations to UL
- no disciplinary sanctions

Duration:
- minimum: 2 months
- maximum: 12 months

ADDITIONAL FUNDING

- Students with fewer opportunities: +250 EUR/month
- Travel support available
- Special needs support available

COSTS AT HOST UNIVERSITY

Students do NOT pay:
- tuition
- exams
- lab or library usage

Possible small fees:
- insurance
- student organizations
- materials

LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS

- OLS (Online Linguistic Support) is optional
- Some universities may still require certificates (IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge)

AFTER RETURN

Students must:
1. Submit documents via UL portal
2. Submit documents in Studis (FRI system)

Deadline:
- within 3 months after exchange
- or before end of academic year

HELP AND SUPPORT

If problems arise, students can contact:
- host institution coordinator
- Erasmus mentor
- FRI coordinator
- UL international office
- student tutors
