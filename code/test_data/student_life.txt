Student Life at UL FRI for International Students

Ljubljana is the capital of Slovenia and a very student-friendly city. The University of Ljubljana has over 40,000 students.

International students can apply for a place in student dormitories (student dorms) managed by the University. The cost is approximately 150-250 EUR per month. Applications for dormitories open in June each year.

The Erasmus Student Network (ESN) Ljubljana organises regular events for international students including city tours, hiking trips, and social gatherings. Their website is located at https://esn-sou-lj.si/. It is highly recommended to apply for ESNcard with more information available at https://esn-sou-lj.si/esn-card/, as well as Ljubljana Erasmus discount card at https://esn-sou-lj.si/ljubljana-erasmus-discount-card/.

Popular nightclubs in Ljubljana for student life are Cvetlicarna, Cirkus, Klub K4, Shooters. A place for alternative subculture is Metelkova street.

Public transport in Ljubljana is affordable. A monthly bus pass costs around 20 EUR for Ljubljana. Moreover, for 35 EUR, you can get a monthly pass for whole of Slovenia. Ljubljana is also very bicycle-friendly with a public bike-sharing system called BicikeLJ, which costs 3 EUR per year. Popular mobile travel applications are Urbana and Travana. 

Health insurance is mandatory for all students in Slovenia. EU students can use their European Health Insurance Card (EHIC). Non-EU students must purchase private health insurance, which costs approximately 50-80 EUR per month.

The official language in Slovenia is Slovenian. However, most UL FRI courses at Master level are in English. Many locals, especially younger people, speak English well.
