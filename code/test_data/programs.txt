Study Programmes at UL FRI

The Faculty of Computer and Information Science at the University of Ljubljana offers several study programmes.

At Bachelor level, the main programme is Computer and Information Science, taught in Slovenian. There is no English-taught Bachelor programme currently available.

At Master level, the programme Computer Science is fully taught in English and is open to international students. The programme lasts 2 years (4 semesters) and awards 120 ECTS credits.

The Master's programme covers topics including: machine learning, algorithms and data structures, computer vision, natural language processing, software engineering, and distributed systems.

A PhD programme in Computer and Information Science is also available. PhD students are typically funded through research projects and work closely with faculty supervisors.
