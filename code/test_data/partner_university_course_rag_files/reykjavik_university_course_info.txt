EXCHANGE STUDENT COURSE SELECTION

Reykjavík University welcomes exchange students for one or two semesters and offers a selection of courses taught in English.

Students should use the official Reykjavík University course catalogue to search for current courses and verify course language, semester availability, prerequisites, and department restrictions.

COMPUTER SCIENCE RELEVANCE

Reykjavík University offers a Computer Science MSc taught in English. The programme allows students to customize their studies and includes emphasis lines such as:
- Artificial Intelligence,
- Cyber Security,
- Software Engineering.

The Department of Computer Science works in fields including:
- artificial intelligence,
- databases,
- software theory,
- language technology,
- user interfaces,
- theoretical computer science.

IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS

Reykjavík University states that thesis work may not be available to exchange students unless a special thesis collaboration agreement exists.

Course availability depends on:
- semester,
- department,
- level,
- prerequisites,
- exchange-student eligibility,
- and Learning Agreement approval.

SAFE ANSWER BEHAVIOR

If a student asks about Reykjavík University, the assistant should explain that RU offers English-taught exchange courses and has strong Computer Science areas, but the student must verify current course availability in the official RU course catalogue.

Example answer:
“Reykjavík University can be relevant for students interested in AI, cyber security, and software engineering. Check the official RU course catalogue for current English-taught courses and confirm recognition through the UL FRI Learning Agreement.”
